Kitchen vs. Table

Reflection On 'Kitchen vs. Table' Video

** All answers are based on the video "Kitchen vs. Table" **

My take on the DBC experience:

Being a new "boot" in just the first week of phase 0, I don't feel like I am able to personally answer this question based on my own experiences. Based on the video, the DBC experience will be many things. The experience will be fun, tough, trying, stressful, successful, and overall worth the huge sacrifices and commitments necessary to be a sucessful DBC boot.

In this type of culture, I see myself struggling quite a bit at first, as I am initially fairly stubborn, hard-headed, and stuck in my ways. I believe that after I am able to get accustomed to the culture and environment, I will have a lot of fun, learn a ton, and be able to help my cohort boots partners succeed as well.

Based on what Shereef said in the video about how at the end of the bootcamp, we will only be "...1/2 of 1 percent of a 'good developer'.", yes, my expectations have changed. I was initially under the impression that at the end of the bootcamp, we would be able to apply for jobs as actual developers rather than classifying ourselves as junior or entry-level developers. On the other hand, I now have a better understanding of what it takes to become a developer, and I now know that it would be literally impossible to become a full developer in a 9 week course.

Overall, I am very excited and eager to be participating in the DBC environment!
